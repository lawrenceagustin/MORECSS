POSITIVE PERSONALITY

  • Loyalty means you are committed to them and it is all about honesty and trust.

  • Being Appreciative means that you recognize the worth of your beloved regardless of what they can give in return.

  • Sincerity promotes emotional connection between partners

NEGATIVE PERSONALITY

  • A Non-chalant partner shows a lack of worry or concern

  • A Aloof person will mostly be distant and reserved in a relationship

  • A person being Dull can hinder the growth in a relationship
